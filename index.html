YelpCamp

Yelp Camp is a fun application that allows you to view a variety of campgrounds around the world. Once you login or signup you can begin to create your own campground that includes a title, image, description, price, and location. YelpCamp even allows you to edit and delete the campgrounds that you created. You can also comment on other campgrounds and edit or delete these comments whenever you want. On YelpCamp you will have access to your profile page and if you forget your password, no problem, you can reset it.

YelpCamp is an application built from scratch using the following technologies:

On the front-end I have used HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap 4, and jQuery. On the back-end I used NodeJS and ExpressJS. For datastore I used MongoDB. I have also used a few APIs such as Google Maps. The application was developed mainly on Cloud9 IDE and source versioned with GIT. The application is hosted on Heroku servers and MongoLab.
